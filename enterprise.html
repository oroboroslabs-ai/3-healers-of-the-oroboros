⚠ Medical Disclaimer & Terms of Use

Purpose and Limitations

THE 3 HEALERS (Hygeia, Aria, and Rheya) are artificial intelligence agents designed to provide general health information, educational content, and wellness guidance. These AI agents are intended to supplement - not replace - the relationship between patients and their healthcare providers.

Not Medical Advice

The information provided by THE 3 HEALERS AI agents:

  • Is NOT intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • Should NOT be used to diagnose or treat any health problem or disease
  • Does NOT create a doctor-patient or therapist-patient relationship
  • May not be accurate, complete, or up-to-date for your specific situation

Always Consult Healthcare Professionals

Always seek the advice of your physician, qualified healthcare provider, or other qualified health professional with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition, treatment, or medication. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of information you have received from these AI agents.

🚨 Medical Emergency

If you think you may have a medical emergency, call your doctor, go to the emergency department, or call emergency services (911 in the US) immediately.

Intended Use

THE 3 HEALERS AI agents are designed to assist with:

  • General health and wellness education
  • Information about botanical compounds and herbal traditions (Hygeia)
  • Neuroscience and cognitive wellness information (Aria)
  • General health information and triage guidance (Rheya)
  • Supporting healthcare providers with research and information synthesis

No Guarantee of Accuracy

While we strive to provide accurate and helpful information, AI systems can make errors. The AI agents may produce responses that are incomplete, outdated, or not applicable to your specific health situation. All information should be verified with qualified healthcare professionals before making any health-related decisions.

Enterprise FAQ

Is patient data stored on your servers? ▼

No. We use zero-knowledge architecture. Patient data is processed in memory and never stored permanently on our servers. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) for HIPAA compliance.

What EHR systems do you integrate with? ▼

We support HL7 FHIR standards and can integrate with any modern EHR system including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, Meditech, and custom solutions. Our Hospital and Research tiers include dedicated engineering support for custom integrations.

Can we deploy on-premise or in our private cloud? ▼

Yes. Our Research and Enterprise tiers offer on-premise deployment options. We provide containerized deployments (Docker/Kubernetes) for AWS, Azure, GCP, or your private infrastructure with full support.

Are the AI agents a replacement for doctors? ▼

Absolutely not. THE 3 HEALERS are designed as clinical decision support tools only. They provide information and suggestions that must always be reviewed and validated by qualified healthcare professionals. The AI agents assist healthcare providers with research synthesis and information access - they do not replace clinical judgment or the doctor-patient relationship.

What's the typical implementation timeline? ▼

Standard implementation takes 2-3 weeks: Week 1 for BAA signing and environment setup, Week 2 for EHR integration and staff training, Week 3 for pilot deployment and go-live. Enterprise deployments with custom requirements may take 4-6 weeks.

What support levels are included? ▼

Clinic: Email support (24-48 hour response). Hospital: Priority support with dedicated account manager (4-8 hour response). Research: 24/7 technical support with SLA guarantees. All tiers include monthly usage reports and quarterly strategy sessions.
